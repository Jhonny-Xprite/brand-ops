# Project DNA (North Star)

**Project:** Brand-Ops
**Core Mission:** Empower creative professionals with high-performance, offline-ready management tools for brand strategy and assets.

---

## 🎯 Brand-Ops Identity

*   **Tone:** Professional, reliable, and high-tech (Violet + Gold).
*   **Target Audience:** Brand Managers, Content Creators, and Creative Leads.
*   **USP:** Zero-server dependency with enterprise-grade synchronization.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Constraints (Non-Negotiable)

*   **Database:** SQLite (local-first) - No external Postgres/Mongo allowed.
*   **Sync:** rclone (provider-agnostic) - Avoid vendor lock-in.
*   **UI:** React/Next.js (App Router) - Must be PWA-capable.
*   **Naming:** Use Portuguese (Brazil) for user-facing labels; English for code/metadata.

---

## 🚀 Vision 2026

To be the fastest asset management tool for distributed teams, working seamlessly in high-bandwidth offices or low-connectivity environments.
